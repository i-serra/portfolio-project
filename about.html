About Me

Inês Serra

Front-End Developer and Audiovisual Translator based in Lisbon, Portugal

Hi everyone, my name is Inês Serra. I'm originally from a small town in the center of Portugal, called Entroncamento, but I have been living in Lisbon for the past 8 years now. Since I was young, I was very passionate about languages and learning about new cultures. Therefore, once I had to choose a degree I decided I would persue this passion and got a masters degree in Translation by the Faculty of Letters of the University of Lisbon. During this period of time I discovered Audiovisual Translation and absolutely fell in love with it. I find it incredibly challenging and creative, enabling to always learn new concepts and technics.
Coding is my second love. I have always liked computers and was very tech-savy. I enjoyed learning by myself how to work out every little problem. However, I never really thought of a carer in tech. I just didn´t think I would be good at it, because I thought of it more like a hobby and not a career. Then the pandemic started and I found She Codes. She Codes showed me that coding is actually for everybody and that you don´t have to be a tech genius to understand it.
